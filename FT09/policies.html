MORINGA POLICIES

Moringa does not tolerate discrimination and harassment, including

  • sexual harassment
  • assault
    • exploitation
  • gender-based harassment
  • bullying
  • stalking
  • domestic and dating violence
    •
Moringa will take specific steps in accordance with the law, our values, and this policy. The policy applies to the
entire community, that is, students, staff, applicants, vendors, visitors, and guests. Please closely read the
Community Safety Policy (Links to an external site.) and our Student Handbook (Links to an external site.).
